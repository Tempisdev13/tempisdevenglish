Esteciapp

Context

A well-known owner of multiple parking lots in El Mante requires an application to have better control of the entry and exit of cars from his parking lot

Thanks to this need, Esteciapp emerged as a mobile application that allows you to have:

  • Access control
  • Output control
  • Notifications to warn your time
  • Find out if there are places available to park
  • Expense control

A solution never seen before in the locality was offered, resulting in an Innovative application that is easy to use and accessible to all.

We sought to offer a simple, intuitive and at the same time attractive design for people.
